# Shining Beauty Spa & Wellness Website

## About the Project
This project is a fully customized and user-friendly website for **Shining Beauty**, the most modern and luxurious spa and wellness center in Adana. The site prioritizes customer satisfaction, modern design, mobile responsiveness, and digital visibility.

---

## Contents & Features

### 1. Main Page (index.html)
- **Hero Section:** Eye-catching title and call-to-action buttons with an Instagram video background.
- **Instagram Feed:** Latest Instagram Reels with custom cover images, direct links to Instagram.
- **Blog:** Up-to-date and informative blog posts about spa, skincare, and wellness.
- **Contact & Appointment:** Easy-to-access contact form, address, phone, and up-to-date email information.
- **Map:** Google Maps integration for location display.
- **Footer:** Large logo, social media icons (Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook), quick links, and legal information.

### 2. Images
- **shining-logo.png:** Corporate logo used in the navbar, footer, and as favicon.
- **reel2.png & reel3.png:** Instagram Reel cover images used in the Instagram Feed section.

### 3. Design & Technology
- **Modern & Responsive:** Fully responsive, modern, and elegant design with Tailwind CSS.
- **Fonts:** Google Fonts (Montserrat, Playfair Display, Pacifico).
- **Icons:** Font Awesome for social media and contact icons.
- **SEO:** Advanced meta tags and favicon for SEO optimization.

### 4. User Experience
- **Multi-language Ready:** Infrastructure ready for Turkish and English (can be expanded as needed).
- **Live Chat:** Customer service chat icon in the corner.
- **Quick Access:** Quick links, fixed social media icons, and easy appointment buttons.

---

## Setup & Usage
1. Keep all files in the same directory:
    - `index.html`
    - `shining-logo.png`
    - `reel2.png`
    - `reel3.png`
2. Open `index.html` in your browser to view the website.
3. If you change the image paths or names, update the `src` attributes in the HTML.
